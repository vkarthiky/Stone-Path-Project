Stone Path Project

Empowering Students for Life Beyond High School

About SPP

The Stone Path Project is a free, holistic platform for high school students, parents, and educators. Built around eight pillars, it provides tools and resources to support academics, wellbeing, and future readiness.

Eight Pillars

Self-Discovery

Understand strengths, interests, and goals.

Mental Health

Daily affirmations, mood tracker, and support.

Academics

Homework planner, reminders, and tutoring.

Friendships

Safe groups, chat, and event planning.

Relationships

Guidance on communication and consent.

Bullying Support

Anonymous reporting and coping resources.

Physical Health

Fitness challenges and nutrition tips.

Financial Readiness

Budgeting tools, allowances, and career prep.

Key Features

Task Planner

Organize homework, exams, and reminders.

Mood Tracker

Log emotions daily with simple tools.

Tutoring

Book tutors and join study sessions easily.

Webinars

Join educational webinars on life skills and careers.

AI Assistant

Get instant answers to questions and guidance.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create your free account as student, parent, or educator.

2. Explore Resources

Discover tools, groups, and personalized content.

3. Grow Together

Learn, connect, and thrive with community support.
